Interesting Facts About Giraffes

Giraffes are the tallest land animals in the world. Their long necks help them reach leaves high in trees, making it easier to find food

Giraffes have the unique spotted coats, and no two giraffeshave the samepattern. They can also run upto 60 km/h over short distances.
